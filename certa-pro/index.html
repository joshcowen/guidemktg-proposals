What We Heard

You've got the tactics. The foundation is what's missing.

In our conversation, a few things stood out. You're running Meta ads, direct mail, radio, and email — real spend, real effort. And you're not seeing the return you should be getting out of it. The radio's been a brand play that works for the franchise, not for you specifically. The direct mail conversion is lower than it should be. The meta ads have room.

That's not a tactics problem. Each one of those channels lands on a message. And right now, each piece is being built from scratch by different vendors, without a shared foundation underneath. When the message is off — or inconsistent — the tactics leak. People see it and move on, or they call and don't convert.

Here's what we identified from your situation specifically:

  • Your current ads lead with CertaPro — credentials, ratings, services. Your customer is looking for someone who gets their frustration first.
  • You're spending on multiple channels with multiple vendors, but no single document exists that tells every one of them exactly how to talk about what you do.
  • The franchise gives you a template, and you've already recognized that working outside it is where the growth is for your specific market.
  • You've got 30,000 past customers and a growing email list that could be doing more work — but only if the message is right.
  • Freedom Flooring is a newer company with more flexibility, and the same messaging work would apply there as you grow it.

What You Walk Away With

A 5–7 page messaging guide, delivered in Google Docs.

This is the document that goes to your Meta vendor, your direct mail company, your email writer, and anyone else who touches your marketing. Instead of every channel being built from scratch, they're all working from the same script.

Brand Story

The full narrative of who your customer is, what problem they're dealing with, and how CertaPro helps them win.

One-Liner

One clear sentence that answers "what do you do?" in a way that makes people want to know more. Works in person, in ads, anywhere.

7-Part StoryBrand Framework

The full framework: character, problem, guide, plan, CTA, success, and failure. The building blocks behind every piece of copy you'll ever run.

Empathy + Authority Statements

How to show homeowners you understand their frustration — before you pitch anything. This is what makes people stop scrolling.

Call-to-Action Language

Specific, tested CTA language for both direct asks and lower-commitment entry points — so every ad and email has a clear next step.

Phrase Bank

The exact words and phrases that fit your brand — and the ones to avoid. Keeps your message consistent whether it's you writing it or a vendor.
